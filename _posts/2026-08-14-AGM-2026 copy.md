WICEN WA will hold the 2026 AGM on Monday 14th August 2026.

The meeting will commence at 19:30hrs at the 1st Midland Scout Hall, 10 Helen Street Belvue.

An official email notification will be sent before the meeting with and attached agenda.

If you have any input for the agenda, please feel free to email [cq@wicenwa.org](mailto:cq@wicenwa.org)
