All formal messages should follow a standard format.

This ensures clarity and traceability.

TO:
TIME:
DATE:

TEXT:

SIGNATURE:

## Priority Levels

EMERGENCY  
Immediate threat to life

PRIORITY  
Urgent operational information

ROUTINE  
Normal traffic
