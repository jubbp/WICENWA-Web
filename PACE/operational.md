Once activated, communications must support:

- Voice coordination
- Situation reporting
- Task assignments
- Message passing
- Information distribution

Members may join operations at any time, therefore communications systems must support persistent messaging.

## Operational PACE

| Level | System | Use |
|------|------|------|
| PRIMARY | Internet Collaboration Platform | Incident coordination |
| ALTERNATE | Amateur Radio Voice Nets | Operational communications |
| CONTINGENCY | Amateur Radio Digital Messaging | Structured message passing |
| EMERGENCY | Voice Relay Network | Station-to-station relay |

## Internet Collaboration

Primary operational coordination occurs through an online collaboration platform.

Recommended channels:

#incident-status
#operations
#field-teams
#logistics
#sitrep

Purpose:

incident-status  
Official updates from command

operations  
Operational coordination

field-teams  
Field unit coordination

logistics  
Equipment and resource requests

sitrep  
Situation reports
