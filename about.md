# About WICEN WA

**Wireless Institute Civil Emergency Network Western Australia**

*Trained volunteers linking the community in times of need*

## Our Mission

WICEN WA is a volunteer organization dedicated to providing emergency communications services to the community. We develop and maintain a trained network of skilled amateur radio operators who are ready to support government agencies, emergency services, and the community during times of emergency, disaster, or special need.

## What We Do

WICEN WA provides:

- **Emergency Communications Support** - Radio communications when traditional infrastructure is unavailable
- **Community Event Support** - Communications for marathons, festivals, and public gatherings
- **Training & Development** - Programs to develop skilled radio operators and emergency responders
- **Equipment & Infrastructure** - Maintained radio equipment and communications networks
- **Rapid Response** - Trained volunteers ready to mobilize quickly when needed

## Our Organization

WICEN WA operates as a volunteer organization under the umbrella of the **Wireless Institute of Australia (WIA)**. 

### Core Values

- **Service** - Dedicated to serving the community
- **Skill** - Maintaining high standards of technical proficiency
- **Reliability** - Proven track record of emergency response
- **Collaboration** - Working in partnership with government and emergency services
- **Volunteerism** - Committed volunteers giving their time and expertise

## Volunteer Participation

WICEN WA welcomes licensed amateur radio operators and others interested in emergency communications. Our members include:

- Experienced radio operators
- Emergency service personnel
- Community-minded volunteers
- New operators eager to gain experience

## Affiliation

**Wireless Institute of Australia (WIA)**

WICEN WA operates under and is affiliated with the Wireless Institute of Australia, the national amateur radio organization.
