# Upcoming Events

## Scheduled Events

WICEN WA participates in and supports various events throughout Western Australia. Check back regularly for the latest schedule.

### Event Types

- **Community Events** - Communications support for marathons, festivals, and public gatherings
- **Training Sessions** - Regular training for members and new operators
- **Emergency Exercises** - Drills to maintain operational readiness
- **Meetings** - Regular member meetings and planning sessions

## Event Support

WICEN WA is available to provide communications support for:
- Community events
- Government activities
- Emergency response operations
- Special circumstances requiring emergency communications

### Requesting WICEN Support

Organizations and government agencies can request communications support for events. Contact us for availability and requirements.
